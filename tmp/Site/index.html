    Those Guys In That Band (live!)

    Listen on KSPC 88.7

    Those Guys In That Band have returned to Claremont to play a live on-air set for the college radio station that gave them their first ever play in regular rotation.

    Sometimes called "a cross between Massive Attack and Raffi", or "dancehall punk polka", their music really does defy genre classification.

    Tune in on KSPC 88.7 fm to hear them, and be sure to check out their page on bandcamp.

    Prof. Penprase, Astronomy Department

    Ancient Astronomy: Stars and Stuff

    Prof. Penprase, Astronomy Department

    Brackett Observatory

    Professor Penprase will be delivering a lecture on ancient astronomy for interested students and members of the community at Brackett Observatory. Topics will include old rocks, old stars, really old stars, and old people. The lecture will be followed by a demonstration of the Brackett Observatory telescope.
